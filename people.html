About Dr. Xiaoxiao Jiu

Dr. Jiu is a Research Assistant Professor at the University of Washington's Foster School of Business. Her research focuses on Accounting Information Systems (AIS), with particular emphasis on the application of artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques to financial data analysis, audit automation, and business intelligence systems.

Dr. Jiu joined the AI Community's review committee in 2025. She contributes her expertise in accounting informatics and AI applications to the peer review process for the Society's annual symposium and workshop proceedings.
