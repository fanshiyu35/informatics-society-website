2026

May 5, 2026

Program Committee Announced for 12th Annual Symposium

The Society is pleased to announce the Program Committee for the 12th Annual Research Symposium (December 10–12, 2026, Budapest). The committee includes researchers from 11 countries. Dr. Xiaoxiao Jiu of the University of Washington joins the AI Community review panel, bringing expertise in accounting information systems and AI applications.

April 20, 2026

Call for Papers: 2026 AI Workshop Series

The AI Community invites submissions for its 2026 Workshop Series (July–November, online). Topics include machine learning, natural language processing, knowledge representation, and AI ethics. Submissions are peer-reviewed. Deadline for the July workshop: June 15, 2026.

March 15, 2026

2025 Symposium Proceedings Published

Volume 11 of the Society's proceedings series is now available. The volume contains 24 peer-reviewed papers across four tracks: Artificial Intelligence (7 papers), Information Systems (6), Cybersecurity (6), and Data Science (5).

February 28, 2026

Information Systems Research Forum Announced

The Society will hold a one-day Information Systems Research Forum on October 15, 2026, in Krakow, Poland. The forum will feature a peer-reviewed poster session and invited talks on enterprise architecture, business analytics, and accounting information systems.

January 10, 2026

New AI Community Chair Appointed

Dr. Péter Horváth of the University of Debrecen has been appointed Chair of the AI Community, succeeding the founding chair. Dr. Horváth's research focuses on machine learning applications in biomedical informatics.

2025

December 12, 2025

11th Annual Symposium Concludes Successfully

The 11th Annual Research Symposium was held December 8–10, 2025, in Budapest. 24 papers were presented across four tracks. The Best Paper Award went to a study on adversarial machine learning in financial systems.

September 1, 2025

Membership Renewal Open for 2026

Membership renewal for the 2026 calendar year is now open. Members enjoy complimentary access to the Society's digital library, discounted symposium registration, and eligibility for committee service.

June 20, 2025

Technical Report on AI in Accounting Information Systems

The AI Community published a comprehensive technical report surveying applications of machine learning in accounting information systems, including financial data analysis, audit automation, and fraud detection.

2024

September 20, 2024

AI Community Holds Inaugural Workshop

The newly established AI Community held its inaugural workshop in Budapest, featuring 12 peer-reviewed presentations and a panel discussion on the future of AI in business informatics. The proceedings have been published in the Society's digital library.

March 1, 2024

Two New Communities Established

The Society announces the formation of the Artificial Intelligence Community and the Information Systems Research Community, expanding its scope to address the growing importance of these fields in information science.
